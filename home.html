What is Pawsitive?

Studies have shown that pets can help people struggling with mental health issues. They're loveable, sometimes cuddly, and also can involve getting outside or being active. COVID-19 has definitely affected people's mental health, so we're hoping to help people and help pets by giving them a way to connect!
